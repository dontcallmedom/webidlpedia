Definition

Realm execution contexts tied to the AnimationWorklet Global name use AnimationWorkletGlobalScope as a basis for their global object.

This means their global object exposes the following members:


    undefined registerAnimator(DOMString name, AnimatorInstanceConstructor animatorCtor);

The following interfaces are exposed exclusively in the corresponding realms:

  • AnimationWorkletGlobalScope
  • WorkletAnimationEffect
  • WorkletGroupEffect

  • ByteLengthQueuingStrategy
  • CountQueuingStrategy
